About CauseBuilders

CauseBuilders was founded by passionate students who believe that every nonprofit deserves a strong online presence, regardless of their budget. We combine our technical skills with a commitment to social good, creating custom websites that help organizations reach more people and drive real change in their communities.

Our team is dedicated to supporting small nonprofits across DC, Maryland, and Virginia by removing the barrier of expensive web development costs. We work closely with each organization to understand their mission and create a digital platform that truly represents their cause.

Since our founding, we've helped numerous nonprofits establish their online presence, enabling them to connect with donors, volunteers, and the communities they serve more effectively than ever before.
